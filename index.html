About

I am a master student in computer science at Federal University of Technology (Parana - Brazil). I've been studying multiagent systems (BDI Agents) since 2015 through JaCaMo Framework. Before 2015, I had been working with N-Graphs and tools to visualize graphs through Prefuse and JGraphX. Morever, I lived in the US for 1 year and half to study computer science at State University of New York at Plattsburgh where I had the opportunity to get deep in the Linux Systems and Computer Architecture. Finally, in the master degree, besides the agents, I've been studying holonic systems, and Agent PathFinder (AJPF).
